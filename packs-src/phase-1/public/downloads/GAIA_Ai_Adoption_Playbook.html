GAIA AI Adoption Playbook

Updated August 14, 2025. A short guide to a safe two week pilot and what to measure.

The path

  1. Pick one task that repeats and hurts.
  2. Write one sentence that defines done.
  3. Prototype with your data and tools.
  4. Add guardrails and a simple test set.
  5. Pilot with a small group for one week.
  6. Decide to scale or stop based on numbers.
